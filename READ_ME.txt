App.py is the working application that can be used to launch the web app. It relies on the Templates, forms and other files in order to function.

Dashboard contains visuals of the employees, products, and Sunday data.

Employees, products, and Sunday.csv are CSV files that were moved into the tractors.sql schema. 

EER Diagram is a star schema that shows the relationship between the tables in the tractors.sql schema.

Requirements.txt includes packages to be installed for the web app to function.
